Welcome  to  the  nim  game!
    Rules:
  • In your turn you should enter numbers from 1 to the number you'll choose later. Computer in its turn will generate random numbers in the same range.
  • Each number will be added to the sum of the previous numbers.
  • The first player who will get the sum equivalent the sum you'll also choose will be the winner! Good luck!
Let's start with choosing game mode. You have two options: to choose a mode where the computer plays with random numbers. The second option is a mode where the computer uses winning strategy.
P.S.: There is a big possibility you'll lose after choosing the second mode :)
Let's choose the range of numbers and the final sum. Numbers must be integer. The last number for the range must be larger than 0. The sum must be larger than 5. Enter numbers and press the button.
